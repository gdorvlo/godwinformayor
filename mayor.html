Our Values


MISSION: The Mission of Progressive City is to be fiscally accountable and achieve results that advance the City's interest. It is essential to safeguard the health, cleanliness and safety of the community in ways that reflect the wants and needs of the people who live, work and visit Progressive City. We must create jobs and improve the quality of education for Progressive's children and adults. We must also pursue opportunities to accelerate and expand the economic vitality of Progressive's business community and neighborhoods.


VISION: Our vision Progressive City strives to be national role model for great schools, creating jobs, ensuring safe neighborhoods, continuing to raise academic standards and expectations, and stimulating the economic development with emphasis on small business. Our City is the “Future of Human Progress” in the 21st Century. Human capital development is instrumental in our vision for the next decade to be competitive job hub that provides a vibrant quality of life for all its residents, employees, and visitors
